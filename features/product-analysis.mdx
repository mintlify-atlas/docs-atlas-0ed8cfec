## Overview

The **Product Analysis** page (Análisis de Productos) provides complete visibility into your product portfolio with visual treemap representations, Pareto 80/20 analysis, slow-moving inventory detection, and growth trend identification. This view helps answer: Which products drive revenue? Which SKUs are underperforming? Where are opportunities to optimize the product mix?

## Key Metrics

### Product Portfolio KPIs

**Active SKUs**
- Number of products available in the catalog
- Filtered to active products only
- Total inventory count

**Categories**
- Number of distinct product categories
- Portfolio diversity indicator

**No Movement (60 days)**
- Count of SKUs with zero sales in last 60 days
- Stagnant inventory metric
- Color-coded indicator (green if zero, yellow/red if >0)

**Average Sales per SKU**
- Mean revenue per product
- Portfolio productivity metric

<Info>
The "No Movement" threshold of 60 days can be customized based on your business cycle. Products with no sales in this period may indicate overstocking, obsolescence, or need for promotion.
</Info>

**Features:**
- Two-level hierarchy: category → product
- Rectangle size proportional to sales amount

- Interactive drill-down
- Hover shows exact values

**Insights:**

- Spot portfolio imbalances

**Shows:**

**Typical distribution:**
- **Category A** (top 20% of SKUs): 80% of sales
- **Category B** (next 30% of SKUs): 15% of sales
- **Category C** (bottom 50% of SKUs): 5% of sales

## No-Movement Products

Identifies SKUs requiring attention:

```python
ultimo_venta = df.groupby("id_producto")["fecha"].max().reset_index()
ultimo_venta.columns = ["id_producto", "ultima_venta"]
kp_sin = kp.merge(ultimo_venta, on="id_producto", how="left")
corte_60 = pd.Timestamp(fecha_hasta) - pd.Timedelta(days=60)
sin_mov  = kp_sin[kp_sin["ultima_venta"] < corte_60].copy()
if sin_mov.empty:
    st.success("✅ Todos los productos tienen movimiento reciente.")
else:
    render_ranking_productos(sin_mov, top_n=15)
```

**Why products stop moving:**
- Seasonal items out of season
- Obsolete or discontinued products
- Overstocked inventory
- Poor placement or visibility
- Price too high vs. competition
- Lack of seller awareness or training

**Recommended actions:**
- Run clearance promotions
- Bundle with popular products
- Discontinue and liquidate stock
- Update pricing strategy
- Train sales team on product benefits

<Warning>
Products with no movement for 60+ days should be reviewed monthly. Holding stagnant inventory ties up working capital and warehouse space.
</Warning>

## High-Growth Products

Automatically identifies fast-growing SKUs:

```python
if not kp.empty and "vs_periodo_anterior" in kp.columns:
    crec = kp[kp["vs_periodo_anterior"].fillna(0) > 0.10].sort_values(
        "vs_periodo_anterior", ascending=False
    ).head(15)
    if crec.empty:
        st.info("Sin crecimiento significativo en este período.")
    else:
        render_ranking_productos(crec, top_n=15)
```

**Growth criteria:**
- Increase >10% vs. previous period
- Sorted by growth percentage
- Top 15 displayed

**Strategic actions for high-growth products:**
- Increase inventory levels to avoid stock-outs
- Promote more aggressively
- Train all sellers to emphasize these products
- Analyze what's driving growth (market trend, promotion, seasonality)
- Consider expanding product line variants

## Product Performance Analysis

### Sales Distribution by Category

The treemap visualization provides immediate insights:

```python
visor_kpi/pages/4_productos.py:95
fig_tree = chart_treemap_productos(kp, title="Productos por Categoría — Tamaño = Ventas")
```

**Analysis techniques:**
1. **Dominant categories**: Largest rectangles show which categories drive most revenue
2. **Category diversity**: Number of visible rectangles indicates portfolio breadth
3. **Within-category leaders**: Largest sub-rectangles show category champions
4. **Tail products**: Tiny rectangles may be candidates for discontinuation

### Product Lifecycle Management

Combine metrics to assess product lifecycle stage:

- **Growth**: High sales increase, increasing frequency
- **Maturity**: Stable sales, high volume
- **Decline**: Decreasing sales, fewer customers buying
- **Obsolete**: No movement, consider discontinuation

- **ALT005**: Products with margin below minimum threshold (critical)

## Overview

The **Sales Team View** (Vista Gerencial) provides management with a comprehensive view of the entire commercial team's performance. It displays the complete seller ranking, monthly evolution trends, activity heatmaps, and zone-based analysis to identify patterns, detect underperformers, and recognize top talent.

## Key Metrics

### Top-Level KPIs

Four primary indicators summarize team performance:

**Total Sales**
- Aggregated sales across all team members
- Comparison vs. previous period
- Progress toward team objective
- Compliance percentage

**Average Ticket**
- Mean transaction value across the team
- Indicator of sales quality and upselling effectiveness

**Sellers in Green**
- Count of sellers exceeding performance thresholds
- Percentage of team meeting or exceeding goals
- Quick health indicator of team status

**Total Transactions**
- Number of completed sales operations
- Activity volume metric

<Info>
All metrics automatically filter based on your sidebar selections (date range, zones, channels, or specific sellers).
</Info>

## Zone Analysis

Breakdown of sales performance by geographic zones:

```python
if "zona_vendedor" in df.columns:
    zona_df = df.groupby("zona_vendedor")["importe_neto"].sum().reset_index()
    zona_df.columns = ["Zona", "Ventas"]
    zona_df["Ventas_fmt"] = zona_df["Ventas"].apply(fmt_moneda)
    zona_df["Part. %"] = (zona_df["Ventas"] / zona_df["Ventas"].sum() * 100).round(1).astype(str) + "%"
    st.dataframe(zona_df[["Zona", "Ventas_fmt", "Part. %"]], 
                 use_container_width=True, hide_index=True)
```

**Displays:**
- Zone name
- Total sales per zone
- Percentage share of total sales
- Formatted currency values

<Info>
Zone analysis helps identify geographic opportunities and potential market expansion areas.
</Info>

## Seller Performance Profiles

The system automatically classifies sellers into profiles based on performance history:

- **Estrella (Star)**: Consistently exceeds goals, top performer
- **Estable (Stable)**: Meets goals reliably, consistent performer
- **Desarrollo (Development)**: Shows potential, needs coaching
- **Problemático (Problematic)**: Below threshold, requires intervention

These profiles are calculated in the data generation layer and used throughout the application for contextual insights.

## Filtering Options

Use the sidebar to customize your view:

**Date Range**: Select any period for analysis
**Zones**: Filter by specific geographic zones
**Channels**: Focus on particular commercial channels
**Sellers**: Compare specific team members

<Warning>
When filtering by specific sellers, team-level metrics will reflect only the selected subset, not the entire team.
</Warning>

## Performance Indicators

The system uses color-coded indicators consistently throughout:

- **🟢 Green (Success)**: ≥90% compliance
- **🟡 Yellow (Warning)**: 70-89% compliance
- **🔴 Red (Danger)**: Less than 70% compliance

These thresholds can be customized in `config.py`:

```python
config.py:112
UMBRAL_CUMPLIMIENTO_ALTO  = 0.90  # Green threshold
UMBRAL_CUMPLIMIENTO_BAJO  = 0.70  # Red threshold
```

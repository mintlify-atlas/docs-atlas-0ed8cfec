## What Problem Does It Solve?

Many distribution companies still manage their commercial data with multiple Excel files, manual reports, and hours of work invested in consolidating data that arrives late, scattered, and with errors.

**Visor KPI Comercial** eliminates this by:

- **Automating analysis** — Converts Excel sales files into automatically processed KPIs
- **Centralizing intelligence** — Managers and sellers access their metrics from a single dashboard
- **Anticipating problems** — Alert engine detects performance drops, at-risk customers, and growth opportunities
- **Democratizing data** — Each seller accesses their own individual report; management gets the consolidated team view

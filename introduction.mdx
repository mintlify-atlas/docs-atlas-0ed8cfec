## What is Visor KPI Comercial?

Visor KPI Comercial is a **sales intelligence dashboard** that transforms Excel sales data into automated KPIs and actionable insights. Built specifically for distribution companies, it eliminates manual analysis and centralizes commercial intelligence in a single, interactive dashboard.

<Note>
  Visor KPI Comercial reduces report generation time **from hours to seconds**, converting historical sales data into informed business decisions.
</Note>

## The Problem It Solves

Many distribution companies still manage their commercial information across multiple Excel files, with:

- **Manual reports** consuming hours of operational effort
- **Dispersed data** making it difficult to make good business decisions quickly
- **Delayed insights** arriving too late to prevent issues
- **Human errors** introduced during consolidation

**Visor KPI Comercial solves these challenges by:**

- **Eliminating manual analysis** → Converts Excel sales files into automatically processed KPIs
- **Centralizing commercial intelligence** → Managers and salespeople access their metrics from a single dashboard
- **Anticipating problems** → An alert engine detects performance drops, at-risk clients, and growth opportunities before they impact results
- **Democratizing data** → Each salesperson accesses their own individual report; management gets a consolidated view of the entire team

## System Capabilities

### Business Intelligence Features

**Automated ETL Pipeline**
- Excel ingestion without manual configuration using `pandas` + `openpyxl`
- Automatic detection of nulls, inconsistent dates, and outliers
- Data normalization by channel, zone, and period

**KPI Engine**

Automated calculation of critical commercial indicators:

| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| 💰 Period Sales | Total revenue by segment |
| 📊 Gross Margin | Profitability by salesperson, customer, and product |
| 🎯 Quota Achievement | % of monthly target achieved vs. goal |
| 🌍 Portfolio Coverage | % of active customers over total assigned |
| 📉 Period Variation | Absolute and relative delta MoM / YoY |
| 🔄 Purchase Frequency | Recurrence by customer and channel |
| 📦 Product Rotation | Active vs. inactive SKUs by period |

**Pareto 80/20 Analysis**
- Automatic classification of customers and products into **A / B / C** categories
- Calculation of **Gini concentration index** for portfolio
- Identification of customers generating 80% of revenue
- Detection of low-rotation SKUs with potential stock issues

**Intelligent Alert Engine**

7 pre-configured business rules that trigger real-time alerts:

| ID | Alert | Severity |
|----|-------|----------|
| ALT001 | Salesperson with achievement < 70% | 🔴 Critical |
| ALT002 | Customer without purchase in last 30 days | 🟠 High |
| ALT003 | Product with sales drop > 30% MoM | 🟠 High |
| ALT004 | Customer portfolio with excessive concentration (Gini > 0.6) | 🟡 Medium |
| ALT005 | Margin below minimum threshold | 🔴 Critical |
| ALT006 | Portfolio coverage < 60% | 🟠 High |
| ALT007 | Opportunity: customer with high frequency and low ticket | 🟢 Opportunity |

## System Architecture

Visor KPI Comercial follows a clean architecture with clear separation between layers:

```
visor_kpi/
├── app.py                     # Executive Summary (main page)
├── config.py                  # Colors, thresholds, formats
├── requirements.txt
│
├── pages/                     # Multi-page views
│   ├── 1_gerencia.py          # Consolidated management view
│   ├── 2_vendedores.py        # Individual salesperson view
│   ├── 3_clientes.py          # Customer portfolio analysis
│   ├── 4_productos.py         # Product ranking and analysis
│   └── 5_alertas.py           # Alerts and opportunities center
│
├── src/                       # Business logic core
│   ├── data_loader.py         # ETL: reading, validation, filtering
│   ├── kpi_engine.py          # KPI calculation engine
│   ├── pareto.py              # 80/20 analysis and A/B/C classification
│   ├── alerts_engine.py       # Alert engine (ALT001–ALT007)
│   └── reports.py             # Exportable report generation
│
├── components/                # Reusable UI components
│   ├── kpi_card.py            # KPI cards with traffic light indicator
│   ├── charts.py              # Plotly visualizations
│   ├── filters.py             # Sidebar filters with session_state
│   └── rankings.py            # Styled HTML ranking tables
│
├── assets/
│   └── style.css              # Global dark theme
│
├── data/
│   ├── mock/
│   │   └── generate_mock_data.py   # Demo data generator (Faker es_AR)
│   └── raw/
│       └── mock_data.xlsx          # ← generated locally
│
└── tests/
    ├── test_mock_data.py       # Dataset validation
    ├── test_kpis.py            # KPI engine tests
    └── test_alerts.py          # Alert engine tests
```

**Layer separation:**
- `src/` → Pure business logic, 100% testable and UI-independent
- `components/` → Reusable presentation layer
- `pages/` → Streamlit multi-page view orchestration
